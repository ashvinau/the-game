The Dwarf Helps

The dwarf informs you that you are travelling through the notorious Black Forest, known for hundreds of years for being a refuge for displaced members of society; witches, cast-away warriors, thieves and murderers. He intstucts you to not stop except to give your horse food and water and continue to the town which the dwarf calls "Seldomtown."

Inquire whether the dwarf has a map to get there.
